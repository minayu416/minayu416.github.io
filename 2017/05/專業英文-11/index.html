寫下那篇文章整體摘要

The briefly summary of the listening article.

The article introduce the interview manual, we sum up the summary of each paragraph and make the whole conclusion of the article.

First, the first paragraph is talking about pay attention to conversation, such as the balance of confidence and modesty, smile, speaking speed and body language.

The second paragraph is about the qualify from company and consists of two sub-paragraph: quality and altitude. The company is finding someone loyal and suit for company not just more hard-working or more smart. About the quality, the article emphasizes the contradiction between the academical institution and the workplace. In this paragraph, it also describes the time distribution in workplace. 

Next, the third paragraph explains the “Model Answer” from internet and the forth paragraph gives the comparison between the interviewee’s behavior and the content of speaking.

In the last two paragraph, the article notices the interviewees’s behavior in the waiting room and give the explanation of the natural state. Finally, getting the highly points in English exam not means that you are good at English ability, by the way, we should improve the English ability constantly not just in order to get into the company.
