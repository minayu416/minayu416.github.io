Autobiography 有四段！

上次課堂有說過要請同學們回去想第一段的寫法。

可以以名字為開頭，但就比較普通

For example,

開頭

The Capital city Taipei is my hometown. Modern, challenge, hurry are associated with this city where I Ming-Jung Yu was born.

Besides text knowledge, marketing is my favorite,…

開頭

In the winter, an infant was born in Keelung. This is infant is me, Ming-Jung Yu(同位語) I am from a middle-class family which is composed of six members: my parents, two elder sisters and one elder brother. My father…..

2rd What life means to you?

  1. Are you happy or sad

  2. Do you have a lot of friends or just a few?

  3. How do you make your school days go by?

  4. Do you have a bf/gf ?

  5. What are your favorite places to go on dates?

  6. How long have you been dating?

  7. If you are involved in a relationship, do you think in will last forever?

可以寫跟學校相關，有做過什麼事，當助教或者參加比賽…

For example,

Throughout my school life, I involved in many contests, such as knowledge competition, financial investment competition and power souring with Taiwan trade competition. Besides, I have excellent performance on study as well as have won the scholarship for outstanding performance in the academic year 2012-2014.

Throughout my college life I have not only gotten good grades but also have involved in various societies. Because join in different ..gained……

修過什麼課：taken class

3rd What is your outlook on the future?

In this paragraph you should explain what you think the future will be like. Pick a year and explain how it will be but explain it through your eyes.

  1. Where will you be?

  2. How will you be living?

  3. Will you be married? Will there be any kids? Who will you be married do? What is he/she like? How long will you have been together?

  • 可以寫近期的目標, 考證照, 申請什麼

  • 計劃

Today's Homework

Everyone always calls me “the knowledge enthusiast”, who have highly curiosity in learning knowledge. That is me, Ming-Jung Yu, born from middle-class family which consist of grandfather and parents in Taiwan, Taipei. My parents learned English and Japanese since I was young thus I put emphasize in knowledge around the world. Hence, my favorite is learning different language to discover this mysterious world in my leisure time. In addition, the computer technology is amazing invention in this world in which I am extremely interested.

下週單字

  1. adapt (v.) 適應

  2. adept (adj.) (n) 熟練的 +at 老手熟練的人 +in

  3. adopt (v.) 採納 領養 挑選 +as

  4. dessert (n.) 甜點

  5. desert (n.) (v.) (adj.) 沙漠.荒蕪的 放棄 離開

  6. dissert (v.) 討論 + on/upon

  7. assent (v.)同意+to

  8. ascent (n.) 地位 提升 + to

  9. accent (n.) 腔調

  10. thesis (n.) 論題 命題 論文
