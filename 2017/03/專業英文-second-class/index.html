What is a Resume?

A personal and professional summery of your background and qualifications.

It usually includes information about your education, career objectives, work experience, activities, reword.

Try limiting resume to one page.

This is most important for people with less than ten years experience.

Use no less than 10 for letter size.

Use a readable and clear font.

Print resume on a laser jet printer.

Use Spell Check and triple check for typos.

Time to begin Resume

“Contact Information”

Put your name, address, email address, and phone number(s) at the very top of your resume

Bold your name

＋姓每個字母都要大寫。名字第一個字大寫就好，粗體：可要可不要或者將名字字放大，姓要放在名字的後面，要放英文名請括弧。

＋地址：請去郵局查詢

＋E-mail

＋phone number (如果申請國外工作的話需要寫國際號碼)。

＠只有電話前面需要加Cell/Home:

What is your Objective? Statement…that is (申請目標)

＋An objective statement is the first line on your resume after personal information

＋A sentence fragment with descriptive phrases and minimal punctuation.

＋Include skills, qualifications and strengths which are related to the job you are applying for.

＋Use a confident tone. but don’t be pompous or presumptuous(驕傲/自大)

＋This is your chance to make a strong first impression

Objective Statements

＋Avoid Being Too General

＋Avoid Being Stupid

＋Be clear and concise(明確的)

A position as a copy editor for the Sacramento Bee allowing me to develop my journalism skills

To utilize my restaurant service training as a general manager of Red Lobster

有的人會在Objective 下加Qualification, 針對要求條件一一回覆

Education 學歷

最高學歷：2個到3個，最高學歷放第一個。

For recent college graduates and students applying for internships, this is an important section.

Only include high school if you have not received a higher degree or taken any college courses.

Until your work experience is more impressive than your education, this category should be the second section in your resume

Ex:

MA.(學位名稱縮寫) in(可用逗點代替) Communications(科系), May 2004(畢業時間or起始(起始8月)～畢業，請看畢業證書上的時間，碩士以畢業證書為主)

California State University Sacramento, Sacramento, CA

Deans List, GPA:3.9⁄4.0 (學期成績)
