<p>因為我完全就不懂我什麼時候要舉什麼例？</p>

<p>這份 PPT 寫得很好我先稍微把一些重點筆記下來</p>
<p>The structure of a paragraph</p>
<ul>
<li>Topic sentence 主題句：Stating what the paragraph is about. This would normally, but not always come at the beginning of the paragraph.</li>
<li>Explanation/elaboration + examples 範例解釋+例證：Explanation or definition of any terms which may be unclear. Then provide evidence for any assertions you make: references to the sources you have used; data, statistics and illustrations.</li>
<li>Implications 呼應與強化主張： Awareness of the implications, stress ideas again and wrapping up an argument. ( this part is optional )</li>
<li>Conclusion 結論： Your last chance to persuade your readers to your point of view, to impress yourself upon them as a writer and thinker. And the impression you create in your conclusion will shape the impression that stays with your readers after they've finished the essay.</li>
</ul>

<p>接下來要舉出一個超重要的主題。</p>
<p>Remember S E X I</p>
<ul>
<li>S = Statement 論點 (thesis sentence)</li>
<li>E = Explanation 說明</li>
<li>X = eXample 舉例</li>
<li>I = Importance/implications 結果與影響</li>
</ul>
<p>接下來來舉例：</p>
<p>Do you agree or disagree with the following statement?</p>
<p>Professional athletes such as football and basketball players do not deserve the high</p>
<p>salaries that they are paid.</p>
<p>• Use specific reasons and examples to support your answer.</p>
<p>我的觀點</p>
<p>Disagree.</p>
<p>Professional athletes are not being overpaid.</p>
<p>為什麼我這麼覺得</p>
<p>Supply and demand determine these athletes’ market value.</p>
<p>解釋我的觀點</p>
<p>Professional athletes provide a service: they display superb skills and stunning athletic prowess for crowds of people to enjoy. As long as people are willing to spend top dollar to attend sporting events, who can say that their high salaries are underserved?</p>
<p>進一步舉例說明</p>
<p>And let’s not forget that sports tournaments are broadcast all over the world via satellite and the Internet, and these channels generate massive revenues for sports clubs, sponsors and television companies.</p>
<p>這個觀點為什麼重要/有什麼</p>
<p>意義</p>
<p>I think it is fair for great athletes to share the proceeds from their companies’ commercial success.</p>

<p>How to write authoritatively？</p>
<p>Which sentence sounds more</p>
<p>authoritative?</p>
<ul>
<li>Many believe that climate change is real.</li>
<li>Climate change is real.</li>
</ul>
<p>Which sentences sound more authoritative?</p>
<ul>
<li>It is my opinion that the Bears will win the game.</li>
<li>The Bears will win the game</li>
</ul>
<p>Eliminate Qualifiers</p>
<p>少使用限定句或是限定物</p>
<p>"The Elements of Style" by William Strunk and E.B. White - assert the facts without qualification.</p>
<p>•(X) Many believe that climate change is real.</p>
<p>•(O)Climate change is real.</p>

<p>Insight 個人觀點與洞察 Insight is the personal understanding gained as a result of coming in contact with particular information. Insight is the understanding gained from your own point of view. It is knowledge or information mixed with your personal experience.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Simplicity 言簡意賅 Simplicity is about presenting information in an easy-to-understand manner. Simplicity is about making the information easily memorable by breaking it down from a complex whole to tiny understandable bits. Simplicity is the evidence of insight.</p>

<p>Depth 深度 Depth simply refers to how detailed your content is. Depth is about how well you’re driving home your point.</p>
</li>

</li>
