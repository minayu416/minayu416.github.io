期末考:

40%

參照： Cover Letter 第一二段

I watched this advertisement on 地方, responding to your ad in 職業 for a position as 職位. I consider that I am the person, you are looking for. My experience and education make me an excellent candidate for this position.

At noted on my resume. I study ＿主科＿ at school, and majoring in ＿主科或者細目＿.  My project experience in school, combined with my strong ＿什麼的知識＿ knowledge and ＿什麼的能力＿ ability, show that I possess the skills needed to be a candidate for the ＿職位＿ position. I am outgoing, hardworking, and easy to get along with. I am willing to learn any new skills needed for this position.

紅字為可刪除懶人包，就是你可以把紅字部分刪掉不寫

30%

我好像忘了在講什麼

大致的Title如下

  • How should the job applicant speak?

好像是，要謙虛，溝通時要有好的手勢動作，反正能想到的跟面試有關，正常的內容都會出現在此段(好不負責任XD)

  1. modest 謙虛

  2. gesture 手勢

  3. persuasive 說服力

  4. exaggerated 誇張的

  5. bouncing 抖腳

  6. reveal 揭示揭穿

  7. condescending 輕忽

  • In the view, what qualites are you looking for?

  • Quality

有點忘了，這兩大段應該是說雖然好的學歷跟學校的表現很重要

但是最好的員工是能犧牲他的時間奉獻給工作(能懂得管理自我的時間)

  1. recruiter 招募者

  2. Academic excellence means having curiosity and the mental capacity to satisfy that curiosity.

  • Attitude

  1. sacrifice 犧牲

  2. invest 投資

  3. be aware of 意識到

  4. as nearly = almost = closely 幾乎的

  • what kind of answers is common interview questions are not effective?

  • what kind of answers are likely to impress you?

  • What are some of the most common mistakes the job applicants make in interview?

ˋ這邊不知道有沒有搞錯，基本上，就是說會有些拼字上的問題或者是沒好好查清楚公司的資訊還有沒有好好看清楚準備的文件

還有一段是說在等候室的時候，都是開放空間，所以等候是所有的動作其實考官都會去注意，不要在那邊做不雅或者不適當的動作與表現

  1. interviewee 接受面試的人

  2. interviewer 面試官

  3. inter 字首 互相

  4. informal formal

  • What kind of English language skills do you look for in interview?

多益可能重要，但是好的成績不代表你的英文能力真的到位

不要只為了進公司而去準備英文，應該是進了公司以後還要持續增進英文能力
