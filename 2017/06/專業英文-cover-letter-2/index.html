There are four paragraph in the cover letter.

  • Personal Information

  • Date

  • Boss/Company Information

Four paragraph

The first to third paragraph:

  • reason for writing

  • point out qualifications(who am I, Brief summary, why do you hire me)

  • call attention to resume, reiterate interest(what is your next step, talking about future)

Each paragraph need 2-4 sentence, the first paragraph can be short and emphasizing the second and third paragraph.

The fourth paragraph:

  • Ask for an interview

  • Indicate your next action

  • Appropriate closing line, such as: Sincerely, Truly yours,

  • Typed name

  • Enclosure(Enclosure: resume, autobiography)

  • cc: (indicated to whom a copy has been sent)

  • Thank them for considering your application, tell them that you have attached a copy of your resume, and when you would be available for an interview. Provide your contact details(Phone/Fax/Email)

  • Yours sincerely,

  • Your signature

  • Print name

The last paragraph approximately 2-4 sentence.

Use

Active / Passive

  • Active: Pat Smith wrote the report

  • Passive The report was written by Pat Smith

容易搞錯的過去式

  • Lay/laid/laid

  • lie/lay/lain

  • Affect: as a verb, means “influence” 影響

  • Effect: as a verb, mean to “bring about”. As a noun, effect means “results”. 導致一個result.

  • All together means “in a group”. 一個團體在一起

  • Altogether is an adverb meaning “entirely”. 整體的全部的

  • One brings something to a place where one is or will be.

  • One takes it when one is leaving for somewhere else.

  • Accept is a verb meaning to “agree”.

  • Except, when used as a verb, means to “exclude”. As a preposition, except means “with the exclusion of”.
