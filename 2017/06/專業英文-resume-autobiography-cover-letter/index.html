Autobiography

Everyone always calls me “the knowledge enthusiast”, who have highly curiosity in learning knowledge. That is me, Ming-Jung Yu, born from middle-class family which consist of grandfather and parents in Taipei, Taiwan. My parents learned English and Japanese since I was young, thus I put emphasis on knowledge around the world. Hence, my favorite is learning different language to discover this mysterious world in my leisure time. In addition, the computer technology is an amazing invention in this world in which I am extremely interested.

Throughout my school life, I divide it for three parts. First, the job of research assistant not only implement school website which is the first website I made, but also discover the interest for website design. Therefore, I designed my website and operate it everyday. In addition, since interesting for article writing and logic thinking between different language and culture, I learn Japanese and English in leisure time. Moreover, I enjoy to communicating with foreigners and receive the knowledge around the world. Last but not least, I won the swimming competition in freshmen time. Furthermore, I joined the swimming training and got certificate of life guard in summer vacation.

To schedule future plan, I extend it for two points. Professionally, enhancing the website design ability is the important point, including different platform, architecture and programming language. Moreover, learning Python and tracing the new trend of information technology: Big Data. In addition, due to the weakness of speaking ability, I sometimes misunderstand other foreigners thus the second important point is improving the language ability.

To sum up, “opportunity favors the prepared mind” is my motto. I can confidently say that I am ready for my future. On the other words, I am looking for a good opportunity in which I can make contribution.
