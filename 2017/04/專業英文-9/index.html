4rd Conclusion (大約三句)

In the conclusion you usually try to re-word the introduction and add some type of closure to bring the whole autobiography together.

ex: In brief, I am sure about myself and always persisting in carrying out my plan of future. To reach my goal, I have tried my best through carefully mapping out my schedule with a hope that my life will go just as I want, “No matter do something, just persist it !”, it always encourages me.

  • 不要在文章中與讀者對話，最後不要寫thank you

  • Thus, In brief, To sum up, Above of

ex: “I can confidently say that I am ready for my future.

ex: “Hard work brings success”, my father always tell me. Thus, I always do as much as I can. I believe that the success will be mine one day. Therefore, I am a perfect match between my expertise and what you are looking for.

ex: “Never give up until I achieve the goal” is my motto. Whenever I study, work or do something else, I always seem under the spell of what I do. I can confidently say that I am ready for my future. However, I am looking for a good opportunity in which I can make contribution.

Homework:

Complete the 2rd and 3rd paragraph.

下週單字

  1. behalf (n.)代表;利益;維護;支持

  2. ripe (adj.) 成熟的

  3. weekday (n) 平日

  4. attract (v) 吸引

  5. cheerful (adj.) 快樂的

  6. comfort (v)(n) 舒適;安逸

  7. diligent (adj.) 勤奮的;用功的

  8. postpone (v) 延遲;推移

  9. seek (v) 尋求;追求;試圖

  10. steady (adj.) 穩固的
