Resume

Resume

Personal Information

  • 姓每個字母都要大寫。名字第一個字大寫就好，粗體：可要可不要或者將名字字放大，姓要放在名字的後面，要放英文名請括弧。

  • 地址：請去郵局查詢

  • E-mail

  • phone number (如果申請國外工作的話需要寫國際號碼)。

  • 只有電話前面需要加Cell/Home:

Objective

  • Avoid Being Too General

  • Avoid Being Stupid

  • Be clear and concise(明確的)

有的人會在Objective 下加Qualification, 針對要求條件一一回覆

Education

最高學歷：2個到3個，最高學歷放第一個。

MA.(學位名稱縮寫) in(可用逗點代替) Communications(科系), May 2004(畢業時間or起始(起始8月)～畢業，請看畢業證書上的時間，碩士以畢業證書為主)

California State University Sacramento, Sacramento, CA

Deans List, GPA:3.9⁄4.0 (學期成績)

研究所的先寫

再來是大學

再來是高中

2015-

2015-

2013-

如果只有語言能力或者證照，那就放大標題而不用寫others

Autobiography

  • 1st Who you are in life

  • 2rd What life means to you?

  • 3rd What is your outlook on the future?

  • 4rd Conclusion (大約三句)

When writing an autobiography, you focus on three major things:

  • who you are in life (personal information, 申請工作：education, 強調語言能力)

  • what life means to you (申請研究所：education, work experience, 可寫可不寫：other)

  • what your outlook on the future is (future life, 希望在這份工作或者這個學校的將來學習哪些，將來要做些什麼(類似讀書計畫))

  • Conclusion or brief summary

**1st Who you are in life**

  • I was born (被生出來)

  • 可以舉家庭互動但不要寫太長

  • Don’t forget to write name.

**2rd What life means to you?**

  • 跟學校相關

  • 有做過什麼事

  • 當助教

  • 參加比賽…

  • 修過什麼課：taken class

**3rd What is your outlook on the future?**

  • Where will you be?

  • How will you be living?

  • Will you be married? Will there be any kids? Who will you be married do? What is he/she like? How long will you have been together?

  • 可以寫近期的目標, 考證照, 申請什麼

  • 計劃

**4rd Conclusion (大約三句)**

  • 不要在文章中與讀者對話，最後不要寫thank you

  • Thus, In brief, To sum up, Above of

Q&A

**What is a Resume?**

A personal and professional summery of your background and qualifications.

It usually includes information about your education, career objectives, work experience, activities, reword.

Try limiting resume to one page.

This is most important for people with less than ten years experience.

Autobiographies analyze and interpret the events in a person’s life.
