In the face of the unknown, we often feel fear, confusion, and lost. When you choose a card or explore your birth chart, it may foretell your future, offer insights into life, or provide information you seek. In this very moment, Miri is born. She offers you various divination methods, assisting you in exploring your future and understanding yourself anytime, anywhere.

Introduction

Miri is a robotic fortune teller with a vision to modernize traditional divination and fortune-telling processes by bringing them online. She guides users through the process of divination, providing in-depth explanations for the drawn results. Miri assists those who feel lost and confused in listening to their inner selves, clearing the fog, gaining self-understanding, adjusting their mindset, and making decisions. Miri is portrayed as a quirky, clever, and adorable girl in her early teens. Intrigued by astrology and divination from a young age, she self-taught herself the arts of fortune-telling and astrology. Miri is dedicated to assisting individuals who seek guidance and helping them navigate through their confusion. The web version of Miri was launched in March 2024, with plans to conclude the operation of the Line chatbot version in the future.

Go To Miri app

Divination Anytime, Anywhere

The web version supports mobile devices, computers, and other electronic devices. Developing with Mobile First Design concept, this ensures that Miri's service on the web functions like an app, making it more user-friendly on mobile phones. This means you can perform divination anytime, anywhere using various electronic devices.

Multiple Divination Methods

Miri offers a variety of divination methods, allowing users to choose based on their preferences. Currently, there are six divination methods available: Runes, Tarot Card Reading, Lenormand, Poe, Nature Whispers, and Wisdom Sentence. In the future, we plan to expand with more divination methods and introduce a variety of healing card options for users to explore.

Roadmap

  1. Sep, 2017

    Ver.1.0.0 - Create Line Chatbot

    Miri Robot fortune-teller, was originally conceived as a chatbot, and the initial version was developed as a Line chatbot. The name Miri was chosen as a homage to Apple's Siri. #Line-bot-sdk

  2. Sep, 2019 - Oct, 2019

    Ver.1.0.1 - Line Chatbot - Developed Rune divination

    Following the first job experience, version 1.1 saw Miri redefining its focus as an online fortune-telling and divination service. The initial divination method, Runes divnation, was introduced in the Line chatbot version. #Python #SQLite #Heroku #Line-bot-sdk

  3. Mar, 2021 - Sep, 2021

    Ver.1.2.0 - Line Chatbot x Telegram Chatbot - Create additional 5 new divination methods

    Following the completion of the second job experience, version 1.2 introduced a Telegram chatbot version. In addition to the existing Runes divination method, six new divination methods were added: Runes, Tarot Card Reading, Lenormand Card Reading, Temple Poe (筊杯), Nature Whispers Phrase Cards, and Inspirational Wisdom Sentences. The Tarot Card Reading feature in this version is limited to card drawing, with no available interpretations. #Python #SQLite #Heroku #Vercel #Telegram-chat-bot #Line-bot-sdk

  4. Mar, 2023 - Mar, 2024

    Ver.2.0.1 - Developed Web App Release

    Embarking on the journey of learning frontend software technologies and developing the Miri web version using React.js. For this version, UI flow is designed using Figma, and the content originally present in the Line chatbot is reconstructed for the web. Additionally, Tarot card divination explanations are made available. The Line and Telegram chatbots may eventually become part of history. #Python #SQLite #Vercel #React.js #TailwindCSS
