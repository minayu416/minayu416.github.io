Reading skills

How do you improve your “reading skills”?

Let’s practice them together.

Why do we read?

英文段落有其固定的架構，是一篇完整文章的基本單位

The three main parts of a paragraph:

1.Topic sentence = introductory sentence
通常只會有一句
主題句告訴我們文章的主題和作者針對此主題的主要看法，也就是文章的主旨。
(the controlling idea = the main idea)

2.Supporting sentences
通常好幾句
作者用承接句”supporting sentences”
提供論點，來支持其對主題(the topic)的看法

3.Closing sentence
Concluding sentence
結句(closing sentence)總結整個段落，有時主題句未寫出，但作者會在結句中重伸主旨。

The three parts of a paragraph

1.Topic sentence – the beginning

2.Supporting sentences – the body

3.Closing sentence – the conclusion

During Reading

skimming and scanning

skimming 快速看過，尋找大方面

scanning 也是快速看過，去找specific information.

skim:
1. title
2. introduction or the first paragraph
3. heading/sub-heading
4. chart photo graphs
5. italic boldface
6. summary last paragraph

scan:
1. specific information
2. 看問題找答案

文章就類似漢堡形狀

期中考

  1. “血型文章”-粗體字單字，或比較重要的單字
    Choose 16分

  2. 閱讀理解
    correct/incorrect 12分

  3. 講過的文法，關係代名詞連接變成關係子句。
    15分5題

  4. choose
    句子 填入是simple/conpoun 對等/complex 從屬/conpoun-complex
    21分

5.標點符號
15分

  1. Reading
    閱讀測驗的回答
    20分

因果關係
because, as, since, now that, seeing that
so, so+adj/adv+that, such…that

比較相同
just as, as…..as/as…so

對比跟讓步
但是/而: but, while, whereas
雖然: though
即使: even if, even though
無論: whoever, whatever, whenever, wherever, however
時間 before, after, when, while, no sooner …than, hardly…when, since, after, and

  2. haul 托運距離 (n.)
  3. browser 瀏覽器 (n.)
  4. diagram 圖表 (n.)
  5. routine 例行公事 (n.) 日常的 (adj.)
  6. system 體系，系統 (n.)
  7. protocol 協議 禮儀 (n.)
  8. flow chart 流程圖 (n.)
  9. accessory 附件 (n.)
  10. request 請求 (n.)
