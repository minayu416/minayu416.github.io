Periods 句點

1.To show that a sentence has ended.
2.To show that a abbreviation(縮寫) has ended.
3.As a decimal point.

Comma Rules 逗點

Sentence Structure

clause 子句

independent clause 獨立子句
dependent clause 從屬子句

片語是沒有主詞的

What is sentence?

  • Subject
  • Predicate: verb & modifiers, object(s), etc.
  • Stars with a capital letter, ends with a period

  • Expresses a complete thought What is the compete thought?

  • It can stand alone

  • It doesn’t need another clause to explain it

  • Certain words can make a complete thought less complete:

  • Example-after, although, as ,as if, because, before, even if, in order to, whether, while.

  • These words can make an independent clause into a dependent clause.  獨立的話是不用連接詞，不獨立的句子要連接詞。

Make sure you know the difference

  • Independent clause: subject and predicate and can stand alone.
  • Dependent clause: subject and predicate and cannot stand alone.

All sentences are not created equal

  • There are simple sentences: one independent clause.
  • “The student in the library read many interesting books.”
  • There are compound sentences: two independent clause joined by a coordinating conjunction(and, or, but, for, nor).
  • “The student in the library read many interesting books, but none provided the information needed for the research paper.”
  • There are complex sentences: an independent clause and a dependent clause, joined by a subordinating conjunction.
  • “Although the student in the library read many interesting books, none provided the information needed for the research paper.“ 
  • There are compound-complex sentences(複合複雜句): two independent clauses and at least one dependent clause. 兩個獨立的子句再加上至少一個獨立子句。
  • “Although the student in the library read many interesting books, none provided the information needed for the research paper, and she began to despair.”

本週單字

Retain (vt.) 保留，保持，記住

She retains memory of her school days.

Remain (vi.) 剩下，餘留，留待，尚待

A piece of pizza remains on the desk.

Explicit (a.) 詳盡的；清楚的；明確的

Can you give me a explicit reason.

Depict (vt.) 描畫，描述

This article is depict that human evolution.

Predict (vt.) 預言，預測

I predict that it’ll rain tomorrow.

Permit (vt.) 允許，許可，准許 

I permit you can come in my house.

Debate (vt.) 辯論，討論，爭論 

They are debating a hot issue now.

Discuss (vt.) 討論，商談；論述，詳述 

They are discussing something now.

Enhance (vt.) 提高，增加 

Sports can enhance body activity. 

Balance (n.)平衡，均衡，結存；結餘 

I lost my balance and fell.
