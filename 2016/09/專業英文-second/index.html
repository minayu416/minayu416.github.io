專業英文-Second

September 28, 2016 in Course Note

今日重點”標點符號”
a,an不定冠詞
the 定冠詞

Punctuation
Capital Letters >> Caps lock

  1. Begin a new sentence
    2.name of ppl,book
    3.專有名詞

Full stops / End marks / Period 句號
1.句子結束
2. 當我們描述完一件事

Commas 逗點
1.separate items in a list.
2.separate clauses 子句，複合句，複雜句

Question marks
We use question mark when a question is asked.

Speech marks
speech marks around the spoken words.
需要一空格 “ a large sheep ”
標點符號都一樣

Exclamation marks 驚嘆號
強調

Semicolon 分號
Apostrophes 撇號

Use commas to connect introductory, interrupting, and some concluding material.
在插入的時候。
Interrupting material will include a comma before and a comma after.

介系詞＋名詞 可以當adj,adv使用

副詞放在助動詞後，___，本動詞前。

conjunction 連接詞= and,but,for,or,nor,yet,so

有分成重數連接詞跟對等連接詞。

ex: because 重數連接詞

兩樣東西只要用 and 連接，但是超過三樣，就要用逗點分割。

Semicolon 分號
可以取代逗點＋一個連接詞。

such as 可以用 for example 代替

Apostrophe 撇號

1.縮寫
2.所有格

she’d = she would

should’ve = should have

absent > adj.

such as 前面用逗點

介紹單字：

  1. mainstream (n.) 主流
    Nowadays, the mainstream of information technology is Big Data.
  2. ensure (v.) 確保
    You need to ensure that you are pregnant.
  3. multiple (adj.) 多個的 (n.) 倍數
    Life is a multiple choice.
  4. application (n.) 申請
    If you want to fix your brain, just fill out this application.
  5. generate (v.) 創造
    The heater is generating heat now.
  6. behavior (n.) 行為
    His behavior make me want to punch him.
  7. digital (adj.) 數位的
    I just buy a Digital camera last week.
  8. minimal (adj.) 極為的
    How to satisfy all demand at minimal costs.9. variable (n.) 變數
    You need to define a variable and coding it.
  9. recreation (n.) 重現
    We want to make recreation of old movies.

#不負責任造句

抽五個單字，會念
1.拼出來
2.造句
盡量詞性造句要跟同學介紹的一樣。
